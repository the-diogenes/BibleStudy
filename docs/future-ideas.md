# Future feature ideas

This is a parking lot for later. None of these are commitments; they are just good
ideas to revisit after the core group, reading, study-plan, and discussion flows
settle down.

## Group Life

- **Prayer board.** Group prayer requests with private/group visibility, answered
  prayer status, and optional meeting reminders.
- **Question parking lot.** Members can submit questions during the week; group
  admins can mark questions for the next meeting.
- **Meeting recap.** After a meeting, group admins can post a summary, key verses,
  action items, and prayer requests.
- **Anonymous questions.** For sensitive questions, members can ask without their
  name showing to the group.
- **Attendance / RSVP history.** Simple planning tool for group leaders, not a
  surveillance feature.
- **Moderation tools.** Group admins can archive threads, hide comments, or lock
  a discussion if needed.

## Study Tools

- **Leader prep mode.** Group admins draft notes, questions, and cross references
  privately, then publish them to the group.
- **Reading plans per group.** Optional structured plans like "Read Luke by
  Easter" without replacing the custom curriculum.
- **Verse memory cards.** Group-selected memory verses with flashcard-style review.
- **Cross-reference explorer.** Tap a verse to see related passages, Old Testament
  quotations, prophecy fulfillment, and parallel passages.
- **Translation comparison.** Stack KJV/WEB/BSB/NET for the same passage.
- **Original-language word studies.** Save a Greek/Hebrew word, see where it
  appears elsewhere, and build notes around it.
- **Doctrine/topic notebooks.** Group-curated pages for topics such as Baptism,
  Grace, Sabbath, the Kingdom of God, or the Holy Spirit.
- **"I'm confused" marker.** Members can mark passages they want help with; group
  admins can see which verses need attention.

## Personal Growth

- **Gentle habit tracking.** Show things like chapters read this week without
  turning Bible reading into a shallow game.
- **Personal spiritual journal.** Private reflections separate from group notes.
- **Sermon notes mode.** A quick Sunday notes page where verses can be linked as
  you type and shared to a group afterward.
- **Notification preferences.** Members choose meeting reminders, replies to their
  comments, or a weekly digest.
- **Audio Bible integration.** Listen to a chapter while reading.

## Maps, Timelines, And Exploration

- **Jesus's life map.** A Near East map tracing major moments in Jesus's life, with
  Gospel references attached to locations and events.
- **Gospel harmony timeline.** Parallel Matthew/Mark/Luke/John events in sequence.
- **Paul's missionary journeys.** Map routes with Acts references and related
  epistles.
- **Biblical chronology views.** Kings, prophets, exile/return, and major covenant
  events.

## Sharing, Export, And Polish

- **Weekly digest.** Automatic recap of new notes, discussions, prayer requests,
  and upcoming meetings.
- **Guest/read-only links.** Share a study plan publicly while keeping comments
  private.
- **Shared resource library.** Links to sermons, books, videos, articles, PDFs,
  and other material, tagged by book/topic.
- **Export group study.** Download notes, discussions, and curriculum as Markdown
  or PDF.
- **Backup/admin archive.** Group admins can export a group before deleting it.
- **Install prompt / app polish.** Better PWA onboarding for "Add to Home Screen."
- **Topic heatmap.** Show which books or topics the group has discussed most.

## Payments / Support Notes

- Use creator-friendly language such as **tip**, **support**, or **membership**.
  Avoid charity-style payment language unless operating as a registered
  charity/non-profit.
